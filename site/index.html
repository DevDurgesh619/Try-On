◆ On Privacy

Your photo, your machine.

Reference photos live in chrome.storage.local on your own device. We do not upload, store, cache, or train on them. The single time a photo leaves your device is the transient request to Google's Gemini model when you click Generate. We never see the bytes.

"On your machine. Period."
